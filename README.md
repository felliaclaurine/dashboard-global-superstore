# 📊 Global Superstore Sales Dashboard

An interactive Tableau dashboard project analyzing the **Global Superstore** dataset to explore sales performance, customer segments, and monthly sales trends. This dashboard demonstrates how Business Intelligence can transform raw transactional data into meaningful business insights through data visualization.


## Overview

This project analyzes the **Global Superstore** dataset containing sales transactions, product categories, customer segments, shipping methods, and regional information. The objective was to create an interactive dashboard that provides a clear overview of business performance while helping users identify trends and compare key business metrics.

The dashboard consists of three main visualizations:

- **Bar Chart** – compares Sales, Profit, and Quantity across product categories.
- **Pie Chart** – shows the contribution of each customer segment to total sales.
- **Line Chart** – visualizes average monthly sales trends throughout the year.

## Key Insights

- Technology generated the highest sales and profit among all product categories.
- Consumer contributed the largest portion of total sales.

- Combining multiple visualizations into a dashboard makes business performance easier to monitor and analyze.
